Who we are

We are a cozy 6-bedroom residential home in a quiet Carmichael neighborhood. This is a family run home seeking to provide close and personal care for you or your loved one. We have extensive experience with elderly and disabled care including dementia care.

Programs and services

Our home provides large private or shared rooms with everything that you need. Home-cooked meals, laundry and housekeeping services, medication management, showers, and assistance with any ADLs such as dressing and eating. We have 24-hour pagers and motion sensors for those that are fall risk. Residents enjoy cable television, movies, high-speed internet, card and board games, and more.

All of our meals are home made, ensuring a nutritious and delicious meal every time. And because we are a smaller home, we can customize and individualize the meals accordingly.

Our home also has a beautiful patio where the residents can enjoy the fresh air and talk. Across the driveway (private street) is another home that we know well and where our residents have made friends.
